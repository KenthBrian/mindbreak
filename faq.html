Help & Support

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about MindBreak.

How does MindBreak help with stress?
MindBreak provides guided breathing exercises and mindfulness prompts designed to help students quickly reset during study sessions. Short 3–5 minute activities help calm the nervous system and restore mental clarity.
Is MindBreak free to use?
Yes, MindBreak has a free tier with basic features. You can access guided breathing exercises, mindfulness prompts, and basic mini-games at no cost. Premium features like ad-free usage, more games, and offline mode are available for ₱98/month.
Does MindBreak collect personal data?
No personal data is collected beyond what is needed to create your account (name, email, grade level). All session data stays on your device. Please review our Privacy Policy for full details.
How often should I take a MindBreak?
We recommend taking a short break every 25–30 minutes of focused study for best results. The app includes adjustable study timers and optional reminders to help you maintain consistent wellness habits.
What is included in the premium subscription?
Premium includes ad-free experience, premium mini-games, custom time settings, full personalization options, and offline mode access. Subscriptions start at ₱98/month or ₱1,176/year with a one-week free trial.
Can teachers use MindBreak?
Yes! MindBreak includes a role system that differentiates students and teachers. Teachers can access an optional classroom system to monitor student engagement with the app.
Which devices are supported?
MindBreak is currently available on Android devices. The app is optimized for various screen sizes and Android versions.
How do I cancel my subscription?
You can manage or cancel your subscription anytime through your account settings or by contacting us directly. Your access will continue until the end of your current billing period.
