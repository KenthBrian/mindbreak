Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. Learn how we handle your data.

Last updated: May 2026

1. Information We Collect

MindBreak collects minimal personal information necessary for account creation and app functionality. This includes:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and grade level
  • Usage Data: App usage patterns, session duration, and feature interactions
  • Device Data: Device type, operating system, and app version

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and improve our mental wellness services
  • Personalize your experience within the app
  • Send important updates about the app
  • Respond to your support inquiries

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored locally on your device. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

4. Third-Party Services

MindBreak may use third-party services for analytics and app functionality. These services have their own privacy policies and we encourage you to review them.

5. Children's Privacy

MindBreak is designed for students, including minors. We collect only the minimum necessary information and comply with applicable data protection laws regarding children's data.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of data collection

7. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at mindbreakapp@gmail.com
