What We Offer
Everything you need to
reset and recharge

MindBreak is more than an app — it's a pocket-sized wellness companion built for the chaos of student life.

🌬
Guided Breathing

Structured breathing exercises like box breathing and 4-7-8 technique to calm your nervous system within minutes.

🧘
Mindfulness Prompts

Short, thoughtful mindfulness activities that ground you in the present moment and clear mental fog.

🎮
Calming Mini-Games

Simple, non-stimulating games designed to engage just enough of your mind to let stress dissolve naturally.

⏱
Focus Timer

Customizable study and breathing timers to structure your sessions — work with intention, rest with purpose.

👩‍🏫
Teacher Mode

Educators can access optional classroom monitoring to track student engagement and promote wellness culture.

🎨
Personalization

Customizable themes, gentle reminders, and premium add-ons let you tailor the experience to how you feel.

Our Mission

"Empowering learners to thrive with focus, resilience, and emotional balance."

MindPause believes that every student deserves accessible mental wellness support — not just when things get bad, but built into the rhythm of everyday academic life.

MindBreak was created by Grade 12 IT-MAWD students at STI College Novaliches as part of their business plan, with the vision of becoming a global leader in student mental wellness technology.
