What We Offer

App Features

Guided Breathing

Short 3–5 minute breathing exercises designed to calm the nervous system and restore mental clarity quickly.

Mindfulness Prompts

Quick, actionable mindfulness prompts that help students reset and refocus during study breaks.

Mini-Games

Simple interactive mini-games designed to provide mental relaxation and a fun way to take a break.

Study Timers

Adjustable study and breathing timers to structure work sessions and mental reset intervals.

Reminders

Optional reminders to help students take regular breaks and maintain consistent wellness habits.

Customization

Customizable themes and personalization options to make the experience feel more personal.

User System

Login system requiring name, email, and grade level. Differentiates between students and teachers with role-based access.

Classroom Mode

Teachers can access an optional classroom system to monitor student engagement.

Freemium Model

Free tier with basic features. Premium at ₱98/month for ad-free usage, more games, personalization, and offline mode.
